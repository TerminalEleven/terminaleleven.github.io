Why is the view pointing in the wrong direction?

Follow these steps to improve the accuracy of your view:

  • Remove any case from your device that may cause interference. Cases that contain magnets will cause the view to point in the wrong direction.
  • Calibrate the digital compass in your device: hold the phone in front of you and swing the device in a figure 8 motion for about 30 seconds.
